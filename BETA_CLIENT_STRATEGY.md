## ðŸ’¬ Common Objections & Responses

### **Objection 1: "We already have a CRM/support tool"**
**Response:** "Great! Ivy.AI integrates with your existing tools. Think of it as an AI layer on top of your CRM that does the manual work your team currently does. We're not replacing your CRM, we're making your team 10x more productive."

### **Objection 2: "We're too small/too big for this"**
**Response:** "Actually, [10-50 employees] is our sweet spot. You're large enough to have real pain around manual processes, but small enough to be agile and see results fast. Our beta clients are exactly your size."

### **Objection 3: "How is this different from ChatGPT?"**
**Response:** "ChatGPT is a general-purpose chatbot. Ivy.AI has 6 specialized agents trained for specific business functions - sales, support, operations, HR, strategy. They work together in workflows and integrate with your existing tools. It's like having 6 AI employees vs. 1 chatbot."

### **Objection 4: "We don't have budget right now"**
**Response:** "That's why this is perfect timing - the beta program is 100% free for 60 days. No credit card, no commitment. You get to test it risk-free, and if you love it, we can talk about paid plans after the beta. If not, no hard feelings."

### **Objection 5: "I need to talk to my team first"**
**Response:** "Absolutely! Here's what I recommend: take this 3-min demo video [link], share it with your team, and let's schedule a group call next week where I can answer everyone's questions. Sound good?"

### **Objection 6: "Can you customize it for our industry?"**
**Response:** "Yes, but let's start with the standard product first. 80% of what you need is already built. Once you're using it for 2 weeks, we can discuss custom features based on your specific needs. That way we're customizing based on real usage, not guesses."
