- No flashy effects

## ðŸŽ¤ Voiceover Tips

### **Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Conversational, not robotic
- Enthusiastic about the product
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace

### **Pacing:**
- 150-160 words per minute (natural speed)
- Pause after key points (1 second)
- Emphasize numbers and results
- Vary tone to maintain interest

### **Recording Setup:**
- Use quality microphone (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB, or similar)
- Record in quiet room with minimal echo
- Use pop filter to reduce plosives
- Record multiple takes and pick the best
