Test these elements for each email:

**Subject Lines (3 variations per email):**
- Problem-focused: "You're losing $47K to no-shows"
- Solution-focused: "How to cut no-shows by 89%"
- Curiosity-gap: "The pet business secret big-box stores don't want you to know"

**CTA Copy (2 variations):**
- Benefit-driven: "Calculate Your Savings"
- Action-driven: "Watch Demo Now"

**Send Time (3 windows):**
- Morning: 8-10am local time
- Midday: 12-2pm local time
- Afternoon: 4-6pm local time

### Tracking & Analytics

Every email should include:

- UTM parameters: `?utm_source=email&utm_medium={{industry}}&utm_campaign={{campaign_name}}`
- Conversion pixels for open tracking
- Click tracking on all CTAs
- Reply detection for engagement scoring

### Follow-Up Rules

**If prospect opens but doesn't click:**
- Send Email 2 on Day 3 (stay on schedule)

**If prospect clicks but doesn't book demo:**
- Send retargeting email on Day 5: "I noticed you checked out our demo..."

**If prospect replies with objection:**
- Pause sequence, route to human sales rep

**If prospect books demo:**
- Stop sequence, send confirmation + pre-demo prep materials
