Two Certification Tracks, One Goal: Expertise

BASIC CNC SYSTEM TRAINING

For operators, maintenance technicians, and supervisors who need solid fundamentals

You'll Master:

Navigation of the user interface menus with confidence
System backups and restore procedures
Parameters: meanings, adjustments, and modifications
PLC Programming basics
Machine monitoring and logic analyzer
Editing, definitions, saving, and compiling
Creating, testing, and verifying simple G-code programs
Conversational programming for faster setup

After This Training: Your operators will handle daily operations independently, troubleshoot basic issues without calling support, and understand the foundation for advanced techniques.

ADVANCE CNC SYSTEM TRAINING

For high-level users, CNC integrators, and OEMs who need expert-level control

You'll Master:

Parameters for drives: adjusting and saving with precision
Loading parameters from CNC and PC
Software changes: upgrade and downgrade procedures
Different drives integration: BCSD, AXD, Quercus
Using FGUIM (built-in software) for screen customizations and modifications
High-level language instructions to control program flow

After This Training: Your team will configure and customize FAGOR systems at expert level, integrate different drive systems, modify interfaces, and control program flow with advanced instructions.
